Career Highlights

Career Journey

2002-2003

Sporting CP

Professional debut at age 17, becoming the first player to play for the club's U16, U17, U18, B team, and first team all in one season.

2003-2009

Manchester United

First Ballon d'Or in 2008. Three Premier League titles, one Champions League, one FIFA Club World Cup, one FA Cup, and two League Cups.

2009-2018

Real Madrid

All-time top scorer with 450 goals. Four Champions League titles and two La Liga championships. Four Ballon d'Or awards.

2018-2021

Juventus

Two Serie A titles. Became the first player to win league titles in England, Spain, and Italy.

2021-2022

Manchester United Return

Emotional return to Old Trafford, becoming the top scorer for the club in his first season back.

2022-Present

Al Nassr

New chapter in Saudi Arabia, continuing to break records and inspire globally.
