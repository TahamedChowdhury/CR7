Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

When and where was Cristiano Ronaldo born?

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He grew up in a modest household on the island before moving to mainland Portugal to join Sporting CP's youth academy at age 12.

How many Ballon d'Or awards has Cristiano Ronaldo won?

Cristiano Ronaldo has won the Ballon d'Or (or FIFA World Player of the Year) 5 times - in 2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, and 2017. This places him among the most decorated players in the history of the award.

What is Cristiano Ronaldo's height and weight?

Cristiano Ronaldo stands at 6 feet 2 inches (1.87 meters) tall and maintains a playing weight of approximately 183 pounds (83 kg). His physical attributes contribute to his aerial ability and overall athleticism.

Does Cristiano Ronaldo have any children?

Yes, Cristiano Ronaldo is a father to multiple children. He is very private about his family life but is known to be a devoted father who values family time despite his busy professional schedule.

What clubs has Cristiano Ronaldo played for?

Cristiano Ronaldo's club career includes:

  • Sporting CP (2002-2003)
  • Manchester United (2003-2009, 2021-2022)
  • Real Madrid (2009-2018)
  • Juventus (2018-2021)
  • Al Nassr (2023-Present)

How many Champions League titles has Ronaldo won?

Cristiano Ronaldo has won the UEFA Champions League 5 times:

  • Once with Manchester United (2007-2008)
  • Four times with Real Madrid (2013-2014, 2015-2016, 2016-2017, 2017-2018)

He is the competition's all-time leading scorer.

What records does Cristiano Ronaldo hold?

Cristiano Ronaldo holds numerous records, including:

  • Most goals in UEFA Champions League history
  • Most international goals in men's football history
  • All-time top scorer for Real Madrid
  • First player to score in five different World Cup tournaments
  • Most followed person on Instagram

How can I request an autograph from Cristiano Ronaldo?

Due to the extremely high volume of requests, Cristiano Ronaldo is unable to respond to individual autograph requests. Official signed merchandise is available through authorized retailers and occasional official giveaways announced on his social media channels.

How can I propose a business collaboration with Cristiano Ronaldo?

For business inquiries and potential collaborations, please use the contact form on our Contact page, selecting "Business Inquiry" as the subject. All proposals are reviewed by Cristiano's management team, who will contact you if there is interest in proceeding.

What businesses does Cristiano Ronaldo own?

Cristiano Ronaldo has diversified his business portfolio to include:

  • CR7 brand (clothing, footwear, fragrances)
  • Pestana CR7 Hotels (in partnership with Pestana Hotel Group)
  • CR7 Fitness (gym chain)
  • Various investments in technology, health, and real estate

What is the CR7 Foundation?

The CR7 Foundation is Cristiano Ronaldo's charitable organization focused on helping children in need through education, health initiatives, and sports programs. The foundation works to create opportunities for disadvantaged youth around the world.

How can I donate to the CR7 Foundation?

Donations to the CR7 Foundation can be made through the official foundation website. All donations go directly toward foundation programs and initiatives to help children in need.

What charitable causes does Cristiano Ronaldo support?

Cristiano Ronaldo supports various charitable causes, including:

  • Children's health and education
  • Cancer research foundations
  • Disaster relief efforts
  • UNICEF and Save the Children
  • Various hospitals and medical research initiatives

Is there an official Cristiano Ronaldo fan club?

Yes, the official CR7 Fan Club offers members exclusive content, early access to merchandise, special event opportunities, and occasional chances to meet Cristiano. Membership information is available through the newsletter signup on our website.

How can I get tickets to see Cristiano Ronaldo play?

Tickets to Cristiano Ronaldo's matches should be purchased through the official channels of his current club, Al Nassr. Fan club members sometimes receive pre-sale opportunities for select matches.

Does Cristiano Ronaldo read fan mail?

While Cristiano appreciates all fan support, the volume of mail he receives makes it impossible for him to personally read every message. His team does provide him with selected messages and notable fan stories. Fans are encouraged to engage through his official social media channels.
